Is a Cold Chain Needed?

For long-distance distribution and storage, apples benefit strongly from refrigerated handling because low temperature slows respiration, moisture loss, softening and decay. Very short local distribution may tolerate simpler handling, but time and temperature must still be controlled.

Product Quality from Stakeholder Perspectives

Grower

Yield, colour, size, disease-free fruit, high pack-out and return per hectare.

Wholesaler

Uniform lots, predictable storage life, low shrinkage and dependable supply.

Trader

Specification compliance, delivery reliability, export fitness and low claims.

Retailer

Appearance, shelf life, sell-through, low waste and customer satisfaction.

Consumer

Taste, sweetness, crispness, freshness, safety, convenience and value.
